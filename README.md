# Signal Restore

**Independent support for business phone systems.**

We help small to mid-sized businesses set up, fix, and manage cloud-based phone systems—without the confusion, downtime, or dealing with providers.

---

## What We Do

- **Setup** — Clean, reliable phone system configuration  
- **Fix** — Troubleshooting routing, call issues, and system errors  
- **Manage** — Ongoing system updates and maintenance  
- **Audit** — Billing and system performance reviews  
- **Advocate** — We work directly with providers and MSPs on your behalf  

---

## Who We Help

- Medical and dental offices  
- Legal and financial firms  
- Small offices and growing teams  
- Call centers and support teams  
- Any business relying on VoIP / UCaaS / CCaaS
